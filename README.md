# Supply-Chain-and-logistics-Dashboard
## Key Insights

- 15.17K orders analyzed with a total supply chain cost of $2B.
- 100% order delay rate with an average delay of 8.5 days.
- Average delivery time recorded at 10.88 days.
- High logistics costs observed across Road, Air, and Rail transport.
- Furniture category contributes the highest product cost.
- Inventory imbalance and stock risk identified in key warehouses.
- Dashboard enables monitoring of delivery performance, costs, and stock levels.
